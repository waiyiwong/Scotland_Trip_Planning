🌟 亮點景點深度解析Attraction Highlights Deep Dive

收費 🎟️Paid 🎟️

愛丁堡城堡Edinburgh Castle

坐落在死火山上的宏偉要塞。孩子們可以尋找大砲，下午一點準時觀看的「一點鐘響炮」更是重頭戲。 A magnificent fortress on an extinct volcano. Kids can find cannons, and the "One O'Clock Gun" is a major highlight.

  • 建議提前網上購買家庭票Advised to buy family tickets online
  • 地面有鵝卵石，大輪推車較佳Cobblestone ground, large strollers are best
免費 🆓Free 🆓

The Kelpies 馬頭水神The Kelpies

位於福爾柯克，高達30公尺的巨型閃亮馬頭雕塑！所在公園擁有超大免費遊樂設施。 Located in Falkirk, giant 30m gleaming horse head sculptures! The park has a huge free playground.

  • 現場有大型付費停車場Large paid parking available on site
  • 外觀拍照與遊樂場全免費Exterior photos & playground completely free
免費 🆓Free 🆓

凱文葛羅夫博物館Kelvingrove Museum

格拉斯哥最受歡迎的博物館！除了滿滿的動物標本與懸掛的戰鬥機，中央大廳每天下午 1 點（週日 3 點）還有免費的管風琴演奏。 Glasgow's favorite museum! Features animal exhibits, a Spitfire plane, and free daily 30-min pipe organ recitals in the Centre Hall (1 PM Mon-Sat, 3 PM Sun).

  • 免費管風琴演奏約30分鐘Free organ recital lasts ~30 mins
  • 必看二戰噴火式戰鬥機Don't miss the WWII Spitfire
免費 🆓Free 🆓

羅夢湖 (Luss小鎮)Loch Lomond (Luss)

偽高地探險首選！從格拉斯哥開車僅40分鐘。風景如畫的湖泊，可以餵鴨子、踏浪。附近還有專為兒童設計的「精靈小徑」。 Top mock-Highland adventure! Just 40 mins from Glasgow. Picturesque loch for feeding ducks and splashing. Nearby "Faerie Trail" designed for kids.

  • 體驗高地風情免去長途車程Highland vibes without the long drive
  • 精靈小徑需額外購買門票Faerie Trail requires extra tickets
免費 🆓Free 🆓

波洛克郊野公園Pollok Country Park

位於格拉斯哥市區的巨大綠地，最大亮點是可以近距離安全地觀看蘇格蘭國寶：長著劉海的可愛「高地牛」！ Huge green space in Glasgow. The biggest highlight is safely spotting the adorable fringe-haired "Highland Cows" up close!

  • 導航至 Burrell Collection 停車場最近Navigate to Burrell Collection parking
  • 廣大的森林步道非常適合推車Vast forest trails are stroller-friendly
免費 🆓Free 🆓

Saughton Park 遊樂場Saughton Park Playground

愛丁堡隱藏版頂級公園！擁有超大的共融式兒童遊樂區、踩水池與沙坑，旁邊還有美麗的玫瑰花園。 Edinburgh's hidden gem! Features a huge inclusive playground, splash pad, sandpit, and beautiful rose gardens.

  • 建議多帶一套備用衣物（有水/沙）Bring spare clothes (water/sand play)
  • 園內有咖啡廳供家長休息Cafe inside for parents to relax

🛏️ 專屬自駕住宿推薦 (Premier Inn)Self-Drive Hotel Picks (Premier Inn)

要在這兩大城市找到**平價且附免費停車**的住宿，秘訣是：「避開正市中心，選擇外圍但交通方便的 Premier Inn」。以下為您挑選最適合此行程的戰略性住宿： The secret to finding affordable accommodation with free parking in these two cities: "Avoid the dead center, choose accessible peripheral Premier Inns". Here are our top strategic picks:

愛丁堡住宿：市郊大眾運輸站點Edinburgh Stay: Suburban Transit Hubs

第 1-2 晚愛丁堡大本營Basecamp for Nights 1-2

首選：Top Choice: Premier Inn Edinburgh A7 (Dalkeith)

  • 免費停車：Free Parking: 自駕家庭的首要條件，省下市中心昂貴的過夜泊車費。 Essential for self-drive families, saving on expensive city center overnight parking.
  • 交通便利：Convenient Access: 鄰近 Eskbank 火車站，僅需 20 分鐘即可直達愛丁堡市中心，免去市區塞車之苦。 Close to Eskbank station, only 20 mins to Edinburgh city center, avoiding city traffic.

格拉斯哥住宿：公路旅行樞紐Glasgow Stay: Road Trip Hub

第 3-5 晚格拉斯哥大本營Basecamp for Nights 3-5

首選：Top Choice: Premier Inn Glasgow Stepps (M80, J3)

  • 充足免費車位：Ample Free Parking: 位於格拉斯哥外圍，環境清幽且停車位充足。 Located on Glasgow's outskirts, quiet and with plenty of parking spaces.
  • 完美地理位置：Perfect Location: 緊鄰 M80 高速公路，往返格拉斯哥市中心與羅夢湖 (Loch Lomond) 都極為迅速。 Right off the M80, extremely quick access to both Glasgow city center and Loch Lomond.
